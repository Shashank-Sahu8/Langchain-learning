Cricket is one of the most popular sports in the world, especially in countries like India, England, Australia, and South Africa. It is more than just a game—it’s a passion that unites millions of fans. Played between two teams of eleven players, cricket involves batting, bowling, and fielding, requiring skill, strategy, and teamwork. The sport has evolved over time, with formats like Test matches, One Day Internationals (ODIs), and T20 games catering to different audiences. Iconic tournaments such as the ICC World Cup and the Indian Premier League (IPL) have further increased its global appeal. Cricket legends like Sachin Tendulkar, Virat Kohli, and MS Dhoni have inspired generations, turning cricket into an emotion that transcends boundaries and cultures.
